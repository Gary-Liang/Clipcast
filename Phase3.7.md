## Phase 3.7 Revision: Minimal Clean Layout

The orb isn't working. Let's go minimal and let the captions be the star.

### REMOVE:
- Audio-reactive orb (delete entirely)
- Waveform (already gone)
- Floating particles (too busy)

### KEEP:
1. **Animated gradient background**
   - Slow color shift (dark blue ↔ dark purple)
   - Subtle, not distracting

2. **Captions (THE FOCUS)**
   - Position: vertically centered (not lower third)
   - Large, bold text—should fill ~70% of screen width
   - Word-by-word highlight working as-is
   - Add subtle scale pop on current word (1.0 → 1.1)

3. **Progress bar**
   - Thin line at bottom
   - Shows clip progress

4. **Clip title**
   - Top of screen, first 3 seconds, then fade out

### Vibe:
Think Apple keynote text slides—clean, bold, confident. The words ARE the content. No filler.

### Layout:
- Top 10%: Title (fades)
- Center 40-70%: Captions (big, bold, centered)
- Bottom 3%: Progress bar
- Everything else: Gradient background

This is the MVP look. We can add imagery/b-roll later as a premium feature.
